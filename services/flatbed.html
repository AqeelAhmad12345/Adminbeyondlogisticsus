Flatbed Transportation Services

“When your freight requires specialized equipment, trust Beyond Logistics to deliver safe, efficient, and dependable flatbed transportation solutions nationwide.”

Beyond Logistics provides dependable flatbed transportation solutions for freight that require open-deck hauling, specialized equipment, or customized loading and unloading capabilities.

Whether you're transporting construction materials, industrial equipment, machinery, building products, oversized freight, or other specialized cargo, our experienced logistics team will identify the most efficient and cost-effective transportation solution for your shipment.

Through our network of qualified carriers, we coordinate the movement of freight safely and efficiently while ensuring compliance with transportation regulations and customer requirements.

Flatbed Equipment Options Include

  • Standard Flatbed Trailers
  • Step Deck (Single Drop)
  • Double-Drop (Lowboy)
  • Removable Gooseneck (RGN)
  • Oversized and Specialized Equipment Solutions
